My employer performs a field service we call Smoke Testing.

The purpose of Smoke Testing is to discover defects in the Sanitary and Storm Sewers.

Once we can identify the problems, we recommend to our client ways to fix them and remove the excess water from entering the system.

This saves money for the Wastewater Treatment Plant, and in turn, costs less money to operate - meaning lower bills for Residents.
