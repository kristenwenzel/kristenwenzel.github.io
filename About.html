About Me

I enjoy making maps. I enjoy making YOU maps. Whether it is a social bar crawl map with some of your favorite hangouts, or a detailed business plan for your city's future road projects - I love them all. I wasn't always like this. I didn't find my true calling until my Freshman year of my undergraduate at University of Missouri Columbia, almost a decade ago. I needed a student job, and plotting points for Emergency Response on a map sounded right up my alley. Little did I know, this student job would lead me to a career that I love, and want to improve upon constantly.
